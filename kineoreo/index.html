Some basic Māori

Why should we learn?

Pronounciation

  • Five vowels: a,e,i,o,u and long form ā,ē,ī,ō,ū
  • Seven consonants: h, k, m, n, p, r, t, w
  • Two digraphs: wh, ng

Simple tikanga

  • Tables
  • Heads
  • Mihi
  • Waiata
  • Kai
